Tomato & Cheese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 200g fresh mozzarella or feta cheese
  • 1 small red onion
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and arrange them on a serving plate.
  2. Crumble or slice the mozzarella/feta cheese and add it on top of the tomatoes.
  3. Thinly slice the red onion and sprinkle over the salad.
  4. Add fresh basil leaves for garnish.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve fresh!
