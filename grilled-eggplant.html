Grilled Eggplant with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 500g chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill.
  2. Slice the eggplants and chicken breasts.
  3. Brush with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and grill until tender.
  4. Top with minced garlic and parsley before serving.
